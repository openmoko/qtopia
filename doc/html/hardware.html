Hardware

These classes provide interfaces to hardware.

QBatteryAccessoryAccess to a battery on the device
QBatteryAccessoryProviderInterface for battery devices to integrate into Qtopia
QBootSourceAccessoryInterface for querying the event which triggered the last boot sequence
QBootSourceAccessoryProviderInterface for integrating device specific boot source detection code into Qtopia
QCommDeviceControllerFacilities to control the power state of a hardware communications device
QCommDeviceSessionFacilities to initiate a device session
QDeviceIndicatorsAllows applications to query, enable and disable indicator lights on a device
QHandsetAccessoryAccess to a handset accessory on the device
QHandsetAccessoryProviderInterface for handset devices to integrate into Qtopia
QHandsfreeAccessoryAccess to a handsfree accessory on the device
QHandsfreeAccessoryProviderInterface for handsfree devices to integrate into Qtopia
QHardwareInterfaceThe base class of all accessory classes
QHardwareManagerFinds available accessory providers for a given hardware interface
QHeadsetAccessoryAccess to a headset accessory on the device
QHeadsetAccessoryProviderInterface for making a headset device visible to the accessory system
QInfraredAccessoryAccess to an infrared accessory on the device
QInfraredAccessoryProviderInterface for infrared devices to integrate into Qtopia
QKeypadLightAccessoryAccess to the keypad light on a phone
QKeypadLightAccessoryProviderInterface for keypad light devices to integrate into Qtopia
QPowerStatusManagerEasy access to the power status of the Qtopia device
QSerialCommAccessoryAccess to a serial communication accessory on the device
QSerialCommAccessoryProviderInterface for serial communication devices to integrate into Qtopia
QSignalSourceAccess to information about signal sources on devices
QSignalSourceProviderInterface for signal sources to integrate into Qtopia
QSpeakerPhoneAccessoryAccess to the speaker phone
QSpeakerPhoneAccessoryProviderInterface for speaker phone devices to integrate into Qtopia
QVibrateAccessoryAccess to the vibrate device on a phone
QVibrateAccessoryProviderInterface for vibrate devices to integrate into Qtopia
