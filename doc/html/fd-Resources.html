Qtopia integration guide

Most parts of Qtopia's documentation are designed to provide a reference for developer's. It can be difficult to read these references because they are taken out of context. The context is provided by the Qtopia integration guide. It simplifies the integration process by providing a chronological order of tasks. The various tasks are put into an order and each task is briefly introduced (with more link to follow up). This improves the integration job by assisting during the planning phase and avoids problems later on due to forgotten tasks.

Note that the guide does not provide in-depth knowledge for each individual step. It rather aims to introduce the reader to a particular task and subsequently refers the reader to other parts of the Qtopia documentation and/or external documentation.
