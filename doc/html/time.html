Date and Time Classes

These classes provide system-independent date and time abstractions.

QDateDate functions
QDateTimeDate and time functions
QTimeClock time functions
QTimerRepetitive and single-shot timers

QTimeStringLocalised strings for times and dates
QTimeZoneAccess to time zone data
QTimeZoneWidgetAllows configuration of time zone information
