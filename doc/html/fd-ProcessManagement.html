Enablers: Process Management
Functional Description

The Process Management system is responsible for launching; maintaining running Qtopia system programs, especially those designed to provide services or execute in the background; and for cleanly terminating them. It also provides logging/debugging support.

Features

  • Life-cycle Management of Qtopia Programs

Life-cycle Management of Qtopia Programs

The Process Management system manages the lifecycle of Qtopia programs. Additionally it
is designed to handle those Qtopia programs which do
not follow the pattern of a typical user-initiated GUI application.
