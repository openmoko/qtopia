Pop-Up Calendar

The pop-up calendar is available for many applications where a date is required.

To select a date:

  • Tap the drop-down list or date field in the application screen you are using.
  • Tap the Month drop-down list or use the Left / Right arrow buttons, or first / last buttons to select the month you require.
  • Tap the Year Up / Down arrows to select the required year.
  • Tap the day of the month on the pop-up calendar to select it.

You can also tap outside the calendar to close it without changing the date.
