Call Forwarding

Sets when and how calls to this phone are forwarded.

You can only forward to numbers already in your Contacts list.

Press for options:

Check status... - network will be contacted and the status will be shown.

Deactivate all - all forwarding will be deactivated.

Forwarding:

  • Always - all calls are forwarded.
  • when busy - when you are talking on the phone.
  • when unanswered - when the phone rings and you don't answer.
  • when unavailable - when your phone is switched off or out of range.

See Also: Set Call Forwarding
