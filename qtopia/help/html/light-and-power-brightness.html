Brightness

The brightness of the Qtopia device's backlight can be controlled using the slider at the bottom of the screen. %%PDA%% %%TOUCH%% Press and move the stylus along the slider to adjust the brightness of the display.

@@PDA@@ @@TOUCH@@ %%KEYPAD%% Use to highlight the slidebar, then press to activate the bar. Now use the Left / Right of to adjust the brightness of the display and press to set or to cancel. Use to move to the next tab.

@@KEYPAD@@

Always ensure that the backlight is adjusted to a level that allows you to read the screen without straining your eyes.
