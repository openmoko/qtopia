ParaShoot

Objective: shoot the parachutists before they land.

%%PHONE%%
  • Use the Left /Right arrows of to rotate and aim the cannon.
  • Press to fire the cannon.
  • Press to exit.
@@PHONE@@ %%PDA%%
  • Press the left / right arrows to position the cannon. Press the up arrow to shoot. If three parachutists land the game is over.
  • Tap to start a new game.
  • Tap to exit the game.
@@PDA@@
