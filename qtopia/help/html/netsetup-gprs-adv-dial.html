GPRS Advanced - Dialing

Used to define how your Qtopia device dials into the internet services. Highlight and select to define.

Manual on and off: internet sessions to be started/ended by you.

Manual on, auto off: internet sessions to be started by you, auto closed if left idle.

Auto on and off: internet sessions auto started/ended as required.

  • Idle timeout: no. of seconds device should be idle before ending an internet session.
