Deleting Contacts

To Delete an existing Entry:

%%PHONE%%
  • View the contact to be deleted. (See Viewing Contacts)
  • Press and select Delete from the menu.
  • Confirm the deletion by pressing Yes.
@@PHONE@@ %%PDA%%
  • Tap the entry to be deleted.
  • Tap Contact, then Delete
  • A message box will ask you to confirm your decision to delete the contact.
  • Tap YES to continue or NO to cancel.

This command is also available as a button on the toolbar.

@@PDA@@ %%PDA%%

To Delete Selected Contact entries:

  • Change to the Contact List view
  • Select the contacts to be deleted by dragging the stylus on the list.
  • Tap Contact, then Delete
  • A message box will ask you to confirm your decision to delete the contacts.
  • Tap YES to continue or NO to cancel.
@@PDA@@
