Asteroids

Objective: destroy oncoming asteroids while avoiding collisions.

%%PDA%%

Use turn the ship and OK to shoot. Once moving the ship cannot be stopped until you have earned brakes ().

@@PDA@@ %%PHONE%%
  • Press the button to launch a new ship.
  • Press the button to activate shields (if you have earned a power bonus).
  • Press the left / right arrows to turn the ship.
  • Press the middle button or the Space bar on the keyboard to shoot.
  • Press the up arrow key to make the ship move faster.
  • Press the down arrow to apply brakes (if you have earned a power bonus).
  • Press to exit the game.
@@PHONE@@
