Moving around Screens

To move up or down a screen: use the buttons to move the highlight.

To move from panel to panel: use / to move to a different panel in multi-panel screens.

To turn a check-box on or off: highlight it and press .

To select from a combo-box: highlight it and press to activate, then use / to select.

To operate a button: highlight it and press .
