Picture Navigator

The navigator is only available when the image is too large to fit on the the screen. It appears in the bottom right corner.

The navigator consists of two rectangles. The larger dotted one represents the image. The smaller clear one represents the screen. Use %%PDA%% %%TOUCH%% the stylus or @@PDA@@ @@TOUCH@@ to move the screen around the image.

Some operations will hide the navigator and return it when completed.
