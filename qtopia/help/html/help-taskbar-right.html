Right of the Taskbar

At the far right of the taskbar are a series of icons that represent Qtopia applets. Applets provide information about the running state of Qtopia. These include:

  • a clock, which links to the Clock application;
  • a battery meter;
  • a clipboard and more.

Tap on the Applet icon to get that applet's controls.

Applets can be enabled or disabled via the Plugin Manager.
