Month View

Shows a whole month - events show as colored squares, all-day events show as a shaded day, multi-day events show as a line.

%%PDA%%

To switch to the month view, tap .

View next / previous month: tap the /.

To see a specific month: select from the drop-down menu.

Tap to view January and to view December.

@@PDA@@ %%PHONE%%

To see a day in detail: use to highlight day, then press or the * key.

To see a different day:

  • use / to see next/previous day.
  • use buttons 1/ and 3/ to move a week at a time.
  • use buttons 4 and 6 to move a month at a time.
  • use buttons 7 and 9 to move a year at a time.
  • to get back to today, press 5.
@@PHONE@@

To change the start day for the week: use Date / Time in Settings.
