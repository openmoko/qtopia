LAN Account

Complete the form by entering the information into the following fields:

  • Name - the name you wish to give this account.

Configure IP automatically/manually:

  • Select the appropriate option to indicate whether you want the Qtopia device to obtain the TCP/IP information automatically (the default), or if you want to enter it manually.

Note: If you choose to enter the TCP/IP information manually you will need to complete the IP tab as well.
