Voice Notes

Voice Notes allows you to make sound recordings, such as voice memos.

To start recording: select New, then .

To stop recording: press .

To play back: select a recording from the list.

%%PHONE%%

To delete: choose Delete from the menu.

@@PHONE@@ %%PDA%%

To delete: press-and-hold on an item and choose Delete from the popup menu.

@@PDA@@

To change settings: select New, then Settings from the menu.

Voice notes are stored in Documents in the Recording category and can be played back using the Music application or by selecting a recording in the Documents list.
