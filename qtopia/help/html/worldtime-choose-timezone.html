Choose Time Zone

%%PHONE%%

Press to activate the map, then the buttons to find a city; press again to set the time zone.

@@PHONE@@ %%PDA%%

To set your current time zone:

  • Tap the Time Zone drop-down list, then tap the time zone you are in.
  • If your time zone is not in the list you will need to use the World Time Map to add it.

To add your time zone to the drop-down list:

  • Tap the world icon to the right of the drop-down list.
  • Add your closest city to the list of Preset Cities.
  • Tap to close the World Time Map screen.

Your new time zone will now be available in the drop-down list.
