Volume Applet Test List

Feature Tests

Adjust Volume

  1. Enable Screen Sounds from the Sound Settings application.
  2. Tap the Volume Applet from the taskbar. A slider and a checkbox will be popped up.
  3. Jump the slider down in increments by tapping underneath the slider bar. The volume of each tap will become more quiet.
  4. Jump the slider up in increments by tapping above the slider bar. The volume of each tap will become louder.

Mute

  1. Ensure Screen Sounds are enabled from the Sound Settings application.
  2. Tap the Volume Applet from the taskbar.
  3. Check the Mute checkbox.
  4. Tap anywhere on the display. No sound will be emitted.
