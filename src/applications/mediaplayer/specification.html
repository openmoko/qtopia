Qtopia Media Player Specification

The Qtopia Media Player plays audio and video media files.

The Qtopia Media Player plays audio and video media files. It provides entertainment by stimulating the senses.

Status: BT

Use Cases
  • Listen To Music

    A commuter while waiting for her bus or train and during her commute is entertained by listening to music. She makes a selection of music which she can listen to continuously during her commute. She easily adjusts the volume to changes in surrounding noise levels.

  • Watch News Clip

    A buisness man during his morning commute to work on the train watches a news report he has downloaded earlier. After he finishes watching it, he deletes it from his device.

  • Watch Movie Preview

    A movie fan downloads a movie trailer to decide if she wishes to see that movie or not. She watches the trailer on the device and leaves it on the device to show to her friends to see if they also wish to see that movie.

Features
  • Play Music: Plays MP3 files
  • Play Video Clips: Plays mpeg video files
  • Key Operation: Can be operated without a stylus
  • Fullscreen playback: Video files can be played fullscreen to give the best possible display of the file if the user chooses this
  • Rotation: The video clip is always rotated appropriately for the current playback area
  • Playlist: A selection of video and music files can be made to form a playlist
  • Skins: The user can choose the look of the audio and video buttons
  • Decoder Plugins: New decoder plugins can be installed to provide support for new media file formats
  • ID3 Tags: ID3 tag fields attached to MP3 files can be displayed and edited

Prerequisites
  • Requires decoder plugins: To play MP3 music files and Mpeg video files, the Qtopia Media Player requires decoder plugins for the formats it can support.
  • Requires data to be from a file: The Qtopia Media Player can only open mpeg files. There is currently no streaming support.
