Memory operations

Enter a number, press M+ to save it to memory.
Clear the display.
Press MR to retrieve the number entered.
Enter another number.
Press M+ to add it to the number already saved in memory.
Clear the display.
Press MR to retrieve the total of the two numbers entered.
