Sound Settings Test List

Feature Tests

Enable / disable keyboard sound

  1. From the Sound Settings application, check Keyboard sound and tap OK. A sound will be emitted whenever a key on the keyboard is pressed.
  2. Uncheck Keyboard sound. Keyboard presses will now be silent.

Enable / disable screen sound

  1. Check Screen sound and tap OK. A sound will be emitted whenever the screen is tapped.
  2. Uncheck Screen sound. Screen taps will now be silent.

Volume control

  1. Check Screen sound, move the volume slider all the way to the top, and click OK. Screen taps are now at the maximum volume.
  2. Move the volume slider midway and click OK. Screen taps are now half as loud.
