Light and Power Settings Test List

Feature Tests

Separate configs for battery and external power

  1. Start the Light and Power application from the Settings dialog. The On power source group box contains Battery and External radio buttons. Toggling this option will alter the information displayed in the rest of the dialog.
  2. Note that when Battery is selected, the Suspend after checkbox is disabled. This forces the unit to turn off after the time period specified to avoid running the battery low. This same checkbox is enabled under External power.

Backlight control

  1. Ensure the unit is running on battery power.
  2. Check Battery from the On power source box.
  3. Change the amount of time for Dim light after to 10 seconds and Light off after to 10 seconds. Click OK.
  4. After 10 seconds, the backlight will dim. After an further 10 seconds, the backlight will turn off completely.
  5. Repeat the same tests with external power connected, and check External from the On power source box.

Device suspend

  1. Ensure the unit is running on battery power.
  2. Check Battery from the On power source box.
  3. Change the Dim light after time to 10 seconds, the Light off after time to 10 seconds, and the time to 30 seconds. Click OK.
  4. After 10 seconds, the screen will dim. After another 10 seconds, the backlight will turn off completely. After a further 30 seconds, the unit will turn itself off.

Screen brightness

  1. Move the slider left (dimmer) and right (brighter). The screen will become dimmer or brighter.
