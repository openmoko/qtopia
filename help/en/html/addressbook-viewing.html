Viewing, Beaming and Finding Addresses

Tap the column headers to change the sort order.

Tap a name to see the full address details.

Tap to beam an address. See Beaming.

Tap to find an address. See Searching.

Select the contact type from the combobox at the bottom left of the contact list. This will change the displayed contact information for each entry in the contact list.

Select the category of contact information you wish to view from the combobox at the bottom right of the contact list.
