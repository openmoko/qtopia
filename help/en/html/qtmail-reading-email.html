Reading email

Tap to retrieve email from all your accounts. To retrieve email from a particular account, first tap Mail, then tap Get mail in and the the name of the account you want to check for new mail.

The Messages tab shows the list of emails in your currently selected folder. To select another folder tap the Folders tab. To view all incoming mails tap 'Inbox'. Tap 'Outbox' to view all messages queued for sending.

In the Messages tab you can tap the From, Subject, or Date column headers to sort your email. Read email is shown in black, unread email is shown in blue. Emails that are not yet downloaded are shown in red. Tap an email to view it using the Examine mail dialog.
