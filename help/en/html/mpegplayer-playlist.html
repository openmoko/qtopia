Play List

Tap PlayList|Toggle PlayList and the screen will split to show the play list and the media library. (Tap PlayList|Toggle PlayList again to show only the media library.)

Tap any files in the bottom half, in the order you wish them to appear in the play list. Tap to play all the files in the list in turn.

The order of files in the play list can be changed by tapping a file, then tapping (up) or (down). Tap to remove a file from the play list.
