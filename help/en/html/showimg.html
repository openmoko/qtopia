Image Viewer

The image viewer opens with a list of images. Tap the name of the image you want to see.

Tap and or use left and right on the keypad to view the previous and next image.

Tap to view the image at maximum size. Tap the image to return to normal view. Use left and right on the keypad to view the previous and next image.

Tap Image to get the menu of rotation options; tap one of the options to rotate or flip the image. The up and down buttons on the keypad will also rotate the image.

Tap to begin a slideshow display of all images. Slideshow parameters can be altered from the View menu. Use left and right on the keypad to move through the slideshow while it's running.

Tap to return to the list of images.

Tap to close the image viewer.
