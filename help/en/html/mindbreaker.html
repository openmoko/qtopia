Mindbreaker

Guess the positions and colors of the pegs.

Start at the bottom. Tap and drag colored pegs to the holes in the wood block. Tap a peg to remove it. When the holes are filled, tap the pin area (on the right) to view the results. Each pin means a peg has the right color. Black pins also mean a peg in the right place.

Tap for a new game. Tap to exit the game.
