Business Card

One entry in your contacts list can be set as an electronic business card, which can then be located quickly and beamed to another device.

To create your business card:
- Create and View a new contact entry containing your own details
- Tap Contact, then Set as Business Card

To view your Business Card:
- Tap Contact, then Show Business Card

To Beam your Business card:
- View your business card
- Tap Contact, then Beam Entry

More information on Beaming is available in the general help topics.
