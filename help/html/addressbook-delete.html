Deleting Contacts

To Delete an existing Entry:
- Tap the entry to be deleted
- Tap Contact, then Delete
- A message box will ask you to confirm your decision to delete the contact
- Tap YES to continue or NO to cancel.

This command is also available as a button on the toolbar.

To Delete ALL Contact entries:
- Change to the Contact List view
- Tap View, then Select All
- Tap Contact, then Delete
- A message box will ask you to confirm your decision to delete the contacts and indicate the number of contacts selected.
- Tap YES to continue or NO to cancel.
