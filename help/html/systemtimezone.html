Setting the Time Zone

To set your current time zone:
- Tap the Time Zone dropdown list, then tap the time zone you are in.

If your time zone is not in the list you will need to use the World Time Map to add it.

To add your time zone to the dropdown list:
- Tap the world icon to the right of the dropdown list.
- Add your closest city to the list of Preset Cities.
- Tap to close the World Time Map screen.
- Your new time zone will now be available in the dropdown list.

See also World Time.
