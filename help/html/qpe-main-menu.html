Main Menu

The Main Menu shows the most commonly accessed features arranged in a grid.

Use the directional arrow buttons () to move the focus onto the icon representing the feature you want to use. As the focus moves, the title bar at the top of the screen will change to show the name of the feature associated with each icon.

To activate the focused feature, press Select.

You can also press one of the keys on your device's numeric keypad to directly activate the feature in the corresponding position on the grid.

To activate a feature, simply press on its icon.
