Mindbreaker

Objective: guess the positions and colors of the pegs.

  • Start at the bottom.
  • Tap and drag colored pegs to the holes in the wood block.
  • Tap a peg to remove it.
  • When the holes are filled, tap the pin area (on the right) to view the results.
  • Each pin means a peg has the right color. Black pins also mean a peg in the right place.
  • Press New for a new game.
  • Press Back to exit the game.
