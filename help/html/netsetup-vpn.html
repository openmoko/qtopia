VPN

Used to manually define the VPN settings for your Qtopia device. A VPN creates a secure internet connection between a mobile user and his home or office network. Qtopia can establish OpenVPN connections.

The VPN dialog has four sub-menus to setup a VPN connection:

  • Account
  • Device
  • Certificates
  • Options

Choose the menu option using , then press Select to open the edit dialog, press Cancel to cancel or press to save and exit the VPN details.
