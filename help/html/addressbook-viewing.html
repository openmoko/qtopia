Viewing Contacts

Contacts can be viewed in two ways. Multiple contacts in a list, or one contact at a time in a detailed view.

To view Contacts in a List:
- Tap View, then Contact List

To view details for a single contact:
- Tap the name of the contact you want to view.
OR
If the contact you want to view is already selected
- Tap View, then Contact Details

Buttons for switching between the two views are available on the toolbar.

If a command is not available in one view, you may have to switch to the other view to use it.

The columns displayed in the Contact List view can be modified using the dropdown list in the lower left corner of the screen or the Configure headers command.
