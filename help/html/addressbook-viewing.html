Viewing Contacts

Select the contact you want to view by pressing on it in the list. Highlight the contact you want to view in the list and press View.

Four Three tabs are available for showing different information relating to the contact.

Use / buttons Press tabs to select Overview, Details , Calls or Messages pages.

Use the menu to delete the contact or access other options.

Overview: displays the name and portrait of the contact, along with related actions are likely to be applied to the contact.

Details: displays the detailed contact and allows activating relavant contact information for the contact.

Calls: displays the list of calls to and from the contact.

Messages: displays messages sent to or received from the contact.
