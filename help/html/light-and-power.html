Light & Power

The Light and Power settings are used to configure the power saving functions of your Qtopia device, depending on the power source in use. For example, when using the battery you may want the device to dim the display light after a period of inactivity to extend the life of the battery.

To configure a power mode:
- Tap either Battery or External
- Adjust the Power saving settings as required
- Tap to confirm your choices, or to cancel.
