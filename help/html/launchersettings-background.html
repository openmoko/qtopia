Background

To change the background, make a selection from the style combobox.

Selecting Faint Ruled will draw horizontal lines on the background in the same colour as selected from Appearance.

Selecting Solid Color from the combobox will allow you to choose a color from the color popup. This color will be flood filled behind the icons in the launcher.

Selecting Image allows you to choose a picture to be drawn underneath the launcher icons. The picture will be tiled if it doesn't fit the launcher.
