Month View

Month View shows a whole month - days with events are shown in bold text, and days with all-day events are shows as coloured blocks.

To view a day in detail: use to highlight the day, then press Select. press on the day.

To view a different month: use / to see the previous/next month, or use buttons 0-9 to enter a date (in your preferred format). press the and buttons at the top of the window to move backwards or forwards by one month.

To return to today: select Today from the menu.

To change the start day for the week: use Date / Time in Settings.
