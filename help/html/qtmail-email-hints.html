Email Hints

  • Tapping will toggle the display of all fields on or off (to provide more room to write the message or verify that you have filled out the right fields).
  • Tapping will store the mail in the Outbox folder, while tapping store the mail in the Drafts folder.
  • Tapping Close will close the dialog. If you were editing a mail your changes will automatically be saved. New mail will always be stored in the Drafts folder (unless you press ).
