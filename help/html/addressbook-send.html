Sending Contacts

Contact details can be sent from one device to another in different ways, i.e. via SMS, Bluetooth or infra-red.

To Send a Contact Entry:

  • View the contact to send. (See Viewing Contacts)
  • Press Options and select Send from the menu.
  • Select the method to send the contact, i.e. SMS, Bluetooth or infra-red.

An electronic Business Card can also be transmitted quickly and easily from one device to another using the Business Card feature.
