Writing Messages

Begin composing by selecting New message from the folder list, or press Options while viewing a message list, and select New.

Select the type of message you wish to compose from the list of options.

Composing

SMS: the total number of characters remaining and the number of SMS messages to be sent is displayed in the top right of the display.

Email: to add attachments to your message, see Message Attachments.

MMS: see Composing MMS Messages.

Message Details

After entering the text of your message, press to move to the details page.

To return to editing the message contents, press Options while viewing a message list, and select Previous.

Message details fields:

To: enter the phone number or email address you wish to send to.

To: enter the email address you wish to send to.

If you select , you can select multiple phone numbers or email addresses from your Contacts list.

CC: send copies to others by adding their number or address.

BCC: send copies to others, and hide their addresses from the main recipient.

Subject: for MMS and email messages, you may enter a subject line.

Sending Messages

After entering one or more recipients, simply press Select to send your message.

If sending is succesful, the message will be placed in your Sent folder. Otherwise, it will remain in your Outbox folder until it can be sent.

If you are not yet ready to send your message, you can save it to edit later by pressing Options and selecting Save in drafts.

To abandon the message completely, press Options and select Cancel.
