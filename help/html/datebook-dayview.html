Day View

Day View shows one day's events. All-day events are shown at the top, others against the times on the left.

To edit, send or delete an event: use / to highlight the event, then press View to view event. press on the event to view it. Use Options to choose the action.

To see a different day: press / to move back/forward by one day, or enter the date using the keypad. press the and arrow buttons at the top of the window to move back/forward by one day, or switch to month view and select another date.

To go back to today: select Today from the Options menu.

To switch to Month view: select Month from the Options menu.

Event icons:

  • indicates an alarm is set for this event.
  • indicates a repeating event.
  • indicates this event is tied to a particular time zone.
