Media Player

Browse through your music and videos, create playlists and play them back with Media Player.

You can access your current playlist and saved playlists from the Playlists menu.

Select and hold to queue items in your current playlist. The item will change color once it has been queued.

To save your current playlist press Options and select Save Playlist.

Browse into the Now Playing menu to see the item that is currently being played
