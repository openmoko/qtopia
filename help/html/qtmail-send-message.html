Message Details

After entering the text of your message, you can set certain properties before sending.

To return to editing the message contents, press Options and select Previous.

Message details fields:

To: enter the phone number or email address you wish to send to.

To: enter the email address you wish to send to.

If you select , you can select multiple phone numbers or email addresses from your Contacts list.

To subsequently modify your selection of recipients, highlight the relevant field then press Options and select From contacts to return to the Contacts list.

CC: send copies to others by adding their number or address.

BCC: send copies to others, and hide their addresses from the main recipient.

Subject: for MMS and email messages, you may enter a subject line.

From: for email messages, if you have multiple accounts then you may select which one to send the message from.

Sending

After entering one or more recipients, simply highlight the recipient field and press Select to send your message.

Message Storage

If sending is successful, the message will be placed in your Sent folder. If sending fails, the message will be moved to your Drafts folder. If sending cannot be attempted at this time, the message will remain in your Outbox folder until it can be sent.

If you are not yet ready to send your message, you can save it to edit later by pressing Options and selecting Save in drafts.

To abandon the message completely, press Options and select Cancel.
