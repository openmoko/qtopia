Viewing, Finding and Beaming Tasks

To View Pending Tasks:
- Tap the dropdown list in the lower left corner, then Pending Tasks

To View ALL Tasks:
- Tap the dropdown list in the lower left corner, then All Tasks

To View Tasks of a Selected Category:
- Tap the Categories dropdown list in the lower right corner
- Tap the Category to view

To find a task:
- Tap Task, then Find

To Beam a task:
- Tap Task, then Beam

More information is available on Searching, Beaming and Creating, Editing and Deleting Tasks.
