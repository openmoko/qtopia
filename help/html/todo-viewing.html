Viewing, Beaming and Deleting Items

Toggle between displaying Pending Tasks and All Tasks by tapping the combobox in the bottom left of the list.

Categories can be selected by tapping the combobox in the bottom right of the list. All items can be shown regardless of category by selecting All.
