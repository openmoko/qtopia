Asteroids

Objective: destroy oncoming asteroids while avoiding collisions.

  • Press the "0" key to activate shields (if you have earned a power bonus).
  • Press / to turn the ship.
  • Press Select to shoot.
  • Press to make the ship move faster.
  • Press to apply brakes (if you have earned a power bonus).
  • Press the "#" key to activate teleporting (if you have earned a teleport bonus).
  • Press Back to exit the game.
  • Press the "*" key to pause/resume the game.
