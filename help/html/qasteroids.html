Asteroids

Destroy asteroids while avoiding collisions.

Press the button to launch a new ship. Press the button to activate shields (if you have collected a powerup). Press the left and right arrow keys to turn the ship. Press the middle button or the Space bar on the keyboard to shoot. Press the up arrow key to make the ship move faster. Press the down arrow key to apply brakes (if you have collected a powerup).

Tap to exit the game.
