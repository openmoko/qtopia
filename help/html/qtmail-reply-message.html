Replying to Messages

To reply to sender: press then choose Reply.

To reply to sender, and all other recipients: press and choose Reply All.

To send this message to another person: press and choose Forward.

To send a message to the sender or recipient: highlight an email address or phone number, and press .

To reply to the current message:

  • Tap Mail, then Reply.
  • Enter your message into the message window.
  • Queue the message for sending.

The Mail menu also contains commands for replying to all, forwarding messages and changing the status of a message.
