Brightness

The brightness of the Qtopia device's back light can be controlled using the slider at the bottom of the screen. Press and move the stylus along the slider to adjust the brightness of the display.

Use to highlight the slidebar, then press to activate the bar. Now use the Left / Right of to adjust the brightness of the display and press to set or to cancel. Use to move to the next tab.

Always ensure that the back light is adjusted to a level that allows you to read the screen without straining your eyes.
