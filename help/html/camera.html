Camera

The photos you take with the Camera application are placed in Documents in the Camera category.

To take a photo: press Select. press the Take Photo button.

Thumbnails of the last 5 photos you have taken are shown in the boxes along the bottom of the screen. you can select one of the thumbnails to view the photo.

While viewing a picture, Press Up arrow to resume using the camera.

Press keys 4 and 6 to Zoom in/out respectively
