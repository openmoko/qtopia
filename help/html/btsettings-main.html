Basic settings

The basic Bluetooth control settings are shown on the main screen, in the Settings tab. On this screen you can:

  • Turn Bluetooth on or off by toggling the Enable Bluetooth checkbox.
  • Control whether your can be found by others by clicking the Visible to others checkbox. To make your visible for a limited period, change the Timeout option to the preferred timeout value (in minutes).
  • Change the Bluetooth Name for your . If your device is visible, this is the name that will be seen by others if they are searching for nearby Bluetooth devices.

To find out more information about your Bluetooth device, such as its Bluetooth address, press Options on the main Bluetooth Settings screen and select Other details.
