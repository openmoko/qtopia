Bluetooth PAN Account

Bluetooth PAN Account Help

Used to define internet account details.

Complete the form by entering the information into the following fields:

Name: the name you wish to give this account.

Networks: the list of configurations associated to this account.

New network configurations can be added/removed via the context menu while the Networks list is selected. The following two configuration types are supported:

Client: the device tries to establish a connection with a chosen Bluetooth device.

Gateway: the device will wait for incoming PAN connections from other devices. This mode is also required if this device wants to share its Internet connection with other PAN devices.
