Shutdown

Note: These termination options are provided primarily for use while developing and testing the system. In a normal environment, these concepts are unnecessary.

The shutdown dialog is used to:

  • Terminate Qt Extended
  • Restart Qt Extended
  • Shutdown the system
  • Reboot the system

To use the shutdown dialog press the button associated with the action you wish to perform. A progress bar will be displayed. You may cancel the termination while the progress bar is displayed. To cancel the termination press the Cancel button or press Back.
