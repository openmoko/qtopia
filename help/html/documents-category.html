Showing Documents by Category

Documents can be categorized to make finding them easier.

To view documents in a particular category:

  • Press and select View Category from the menu.
  • Use to highlight the category and press to limit the list of documents to only those with that category.
  • Press to exit.
  • Tap the name of the category of documents to view

Documents that have not been categorized:

  • Tap Unfiled to display a list of uncategorized documents.

All documents:

  • Tap All to display a list of all documents.
