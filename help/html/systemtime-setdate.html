Setting the Date

To set the date:

  • Tap the Date drop-down list to select the current date from the pop-up calendar.

To set the day of the week calendar begins on:

  • Tap the Weeks start on drop-down list, then either Sunday or Monday.

To select your preferred date format:
  • Tap the Date format drop-down list.

To confirm the new date and time tap otherwise tap to cancel the changes.
