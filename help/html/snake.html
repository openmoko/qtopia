Snake

Objective: make the snake grow longer by eating mice and avoiding obstacles.

Press Select to start or to pause the game.

Use the buttons to change direction.

Tap anywhere on the screen to start the game.

The screen is invisibly divided into four triangles, from the center of the screen to the corners). Tap in one of the triangles to turn the snake in that direction.

Try to 'eat' the white mice without hitting the walls.

Every time the snake eats a mouse it will grow longer and will move a little faster.
