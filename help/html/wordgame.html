Word Game

Objective: use letters to form words as efficiently as possible.

  • Select or enter player names using the combo boxes.
  • Tap Start to begin a game.
  • Tap and drag letters to the left to form words to place on the board.
  • Tap the board and drag the stylus across the squares on the board where the word should go.
  • Tap the Tick button to end your turn.
  • Tap the left arrow to remove letters from the board.
  • Tap to end the game, and to exit.
