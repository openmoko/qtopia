Reading Messages

Press / to read a message.

To retrieve email messages for all accounts:

  • Press and select Get all mail from the menu.
  • The messages will be received into your Inbox.

To retrieve email messages for all accounts:

  • Tap .

To retrieve email from a particular account:

  • Use Mail, then Get mail in, and then the name of the account you want to retrieve the messages from.
  • The messages will be received into your Inbox.

To view the contents of your Inbox:

  • Select the Inbox, then the Messages tab.
  • Read email is shown in black, unread email is shown in blue. Email that has not yet been downloaded is shown in red.
  • Select an email to view it using the Reading mail dialog.
  • Use Previous or Next to other messages in the current folder.
