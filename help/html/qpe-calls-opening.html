Making Calls

From the home screen:

To dial: enter the number tap the dialer image, tap the number on the displayed keypad, then press Call.

To speed dial: hold down the number button; for 2-digit speed dial, hold down the second digit number button.

Press the favorites icon on the homescreen to access a list containing your speed dial actions.

To dial a contact: tap the dialer image, spell the first few letters of their first or last name, press , and then highlight and select the contact's number from the list. Press Call to call the selected number.
