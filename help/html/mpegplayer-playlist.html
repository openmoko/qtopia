Creating Play Lists

Songs and videos can be listed in any particular order. To add a single entry into the playlist, select the song or video and tap .

To remove an entry from the playlist, select the Playlist tab, select the song or video to remove, and tap .

The play order can be modified by selecting a song or video in the Playlist tab and using and to move the entry up or down in order. Also the stylus can be used to drag items up and down the order of the list.

Use Clear from the playlist menu to remove all entries from the playlist.

The Add all Music, Add all Video and Add all from the playlist menu add to the playlist the appropriate files visible from the Music and/or Video tabs. Category and location combo boxes located at the bottom of those lists can be used to filter the Music and Video lists.
