View Call History

Shows the details of a particular call, including the time and date the call was made, when it ended and its total duration.

The time zone information at the time the call is made is also displayed if it is different to the current time zone.

Send Message: opens a message composer if the type of number is a mobile number.

Open Contact: opens Contacts to show the details of the saved contact.

Delete: allows to remove the current call history entry.
