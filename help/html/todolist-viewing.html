Viewing Tasks

To View Pending Tasks:

  • Tap the drop-down list in the lower left corner, then Pending Tasks.

To View ALL Tasks:

  • Tap the drop-down list in the lower left corner, then All Tasks.

To View Tasks of a Selected Category:

  • Tap the Categories drop-down list in the lower right corner.
  • Tap the category of task that you want to view.
To view a list of all the tasks, simply open the Tasks application.

View Tasks by Category:

  • Press and select View Category... from the menu.
  • In the list of categories use and to select the category.
  • The next time you launch Tasks all categories will be displayed.
