Retrieving E-mail

To retrieve e-mail messages for all accounts:
- Tap

To retrieve email from a particular account:
- Tap Mail, then Get mail in, and then the name of the account you want to retrieve the messages from.
- The Messages will be received into your Inbox

To view the contents of your Inbox:
- Tap the Inbox, then tap the Messages page

Read email is shown in black, unread email is shown in blue. Emails that are not yet downloaded are shown in red.

Tap an email to view it using the Reading mail dialog.

More information is available on Reading mail.

Status flags

All mail has an associated status:

Normal mail messages.

Unread mail messages.

Messages still to download from server.

Messages still to be sent.

Composed messages that aren't yet finished, waiting to be sent.
