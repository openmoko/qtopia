Recalibrate

To ensure that where you tap on the screen matches the screen contents, you may occasionally need to re-calibrate the touch screen.

  • Select and follow the on-screen instructions.
  • The screen will change and show a cross near the top left of the screen.
  • Press the stylus in the center of the cross, hold for a moment, then release.
  • The cross will then move to another position.
  • Press, hold and release once again. Continue until re-calibration has successfully been completed.

Note: the calibration process will repeat if it detects any inaccuracies.
