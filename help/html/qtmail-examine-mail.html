Examine mail

This dialog shows an email. Tap the up and down arrows to look at the previous and next email. Tap to write a reply. (See the Write Mail dialog.) Tap to move the mail to the 'Trash' folder. If the mail is already in the 'Trash' folder a dialog will ask for confirmation of deletion.

Tap the Mail menu option to reply, reply to all or forward the email. This menu also has options for changing the email's status and for deleting the email.

If you are examining the 'Outbox' folder you can send just this mail by tapping the button. If the mail is Unsent or in the 'Drafts' folder you can edit it by tapping which will bring you to the Write Mail window.

If you are examining the 'Inbox' folder you can complete a mail that is still on the server by tapping

The View menu can be used to move to the next or previous email and to view the email as plain text.

Any email addresses or URLs in the mail will be shown in blue and underlined. Tapping an email addresses will give you an option of either writing an email to this recipient, or adding them to your Contacts. Clicking an URL will open it in the default Web Browser.
