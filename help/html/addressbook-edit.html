Editing Contacts

Four information tabs are available for recording different contact details.

To enter information:
  • Tap the tab you wish to view with the stylus.
  • Tap the field you wish to edit.
  • Select the Input Method you want to use to edit the field.
  • On the Other Details tab you can change the contact's category by tapping the Categories field.
  • Select to save or to abandon your changes.

Use / buttons Press tabs to select General, Business, Home or Notes pages.

General:

  • Name: shows on contact list.
  • Emails: email addresses.
  • VoIPid: Voice over IP identity.
  • Business contact: turn on to show company and title on this screen.
  • Company: company name.
  • Title: job title.

Business: Cmpny, Title show same as on General page. Phone, Mobile, Fax, Pager also show the same business numbers as set on the General page.

Home: Phone, Mobile, Fax show same home numbers as set on General page.

Notes: use this to add free-text notes.

  • Photo: shows on Contacts list, shows when this person calls you. To add or change a photo, highlight and select.
  • Ringtone: will sound when this person calls.
  • Category: used to group this to similar records.
