Email Address Book

Tap the @ icon to invoke the list of names and email addresses available. Tap the names you want to add, then tap 'OK' to add them and hide the address book.

The Email Address Book is automatically updated with addresses as you send and receive mail. It also uses the addresses available in your Contacts. The addresses in the Email Address Book can be deleted by tapping , and they can be added to your Contacts by tapping 'Add to Contacts'.
