Contacts and Email Address List

The Contacts and Email Address list combines entries from Contacts with addresses collected automatically from Email as you send and receive messages.

To address a message to a contact listed in the Contacts and Email Address list
- Tap the @ button to the right of the To, CC or BCC fields
- Tap the name of each contact you want to send the message to
- Tap to close the list and return to the message.

To add an e-mail address to contacts:
- Tap an @ button to access the Contacts and Email Address list
- Tap the address to add
- Tap the Add to Contacts button.
