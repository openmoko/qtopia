Preferences

The time between successive images in the slideshow can be changed by moving the slider. To restart the slideshow from the beginning, check Repeat slideshow. To show the slideshow in reverse, check Show pictures in reverse.

To automatically rotate images to make better use of the available display space, check Rotate pictures to fit screen. By default pictures will be rotated anti-clockwise; to rotate clockwise, check Rotate clockwise.

Check Fast load pictures for faster image loading at a slightly reduced quality.
