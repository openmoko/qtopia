Picture View

You can change how the image is viewed with these operations.

To zoom in and out: press and select . Use the stylus or to control the slidebar. Press when you have finished or to revert to the original size. Tap the image or press when you have finished.

If the image is too large to fit on screen the navigator will appear.

To use full screen: press and select . Tap the image or press Press to exit from full screen.
