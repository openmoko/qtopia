Using headsets and handsfree units

To use a Bluetooth headset or handsfree unit with your , you will need to "pair" with it and then make a connection.

To pair with the headset or handsfree device, see Pairing with other devices. Once you have created the pairing, the device will appear in the list in the Paired Devices tab. Select the device in the list, and a screen will appear with additional information about the device.

This screen can be used to connect to headset and handsfree units. To connect, press the Connect headset or Connect handsfree unit button.

Once you have paired with your headset or handsfree device and connected to it, it will "remember" your for future connections; you do not need to return to Bluetooth Settings to connect to the device. However, situations may arise where you will need to re-pair with a device if it is unable to find and connect to your . In this case, go to the Paired Devices tab, select the device from the list, and remove the pairing by pressing Options and selecting Remove pairing. Then, repeat the pairing and connection process described above.
