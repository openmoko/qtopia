Enter Email Addresses

Enter the email address of the recipient next to the 'To:' field. using one of the Input Methods.

If you want to send to several people, enter each email address, separated by a comma. If you also want to send a courtesy copy or blind courtesy copy, enter their email addresses in the fields next to the 'CC' or 'BCC' labels. If these fields are not accessible, tap Fields and their entry in this menu in order to display them.

To save typing you can use the Email Address Book to enter email addresses.
