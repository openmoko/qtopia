Changing the Preset Cities

The preset cities are displayed below the world map and also in the Time Zone drop-down list on the Set Date / Time screen.

To change a preset city:

  • Tap the city button that you want to change. (The button will stay down and the others will become unavailable).
  • Double click, press and drag the stylus over the map until the city you want appears, then release the stylus.
  • Press the button on the map.
  • The button will now show the new city you have selected.
  • To cancel your selection, press the button on themap.
  • Use to highlight the city name
  • Press Select or use Select City from the menu to highlight the city name and then press Select. This will cause the map to gain focus.
  • Use to move around the map. Holding down will make the cursor move faster.
  • Press Select to select the city shown on the map.
  • The new city you have selected will be shown below the map.
