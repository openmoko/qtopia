World Time Map

The World Time Map casts a shadow across those parts of the world currently experiencing night time.

To zoom in or out on the map:

  • Press the to highlight the button.
  • Press Select to zoom in or out.
  • Press the button in the lower-right corner of the world map.

To use continuous select mode:

  • Double click on the map, and drag the stylus across the map.

To select the highlighted city:

  • Press the button on the map.

To cancel your selection:

  • Press the button on the map.
