Keyboard Help

Pop-up the input area in keyboard mode.

The keyboard works just like a full-size keyboard, except that you use a stylus instead of fingers. As you tap, words appear above the keyboard that you can tap to insert, rather than having to finish typing the word.

The Shift and Ctrl keys are 'sticky'; if you tap Shift, it will remain down until you tap it again or tap a letter.
