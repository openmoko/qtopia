Add to Speed Dial

To add the phone number of a contact to Speed Dial:

  • View the contact.
  • Press Options and select Add to Speed Dial... from the menu.
  • Choose whether you want Speed Dial to call, text, or view the contact.
  • If the contact has more than one phone number, choose which of them to call or text.
  • Choose a speed dial slot for the contact.
