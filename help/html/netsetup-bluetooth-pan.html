Bluetooth Personal Area Network (PAN)

The PAN profile allows to connect several Bluetooth devices together to form some kind of ad-hoc network. To establish connections one device waits for other Bluetooth devices to connect to it. Once more than one device has connected the devices are joined together to form one big network whereby every device can communicate to all other devices.

PAN supports several network configurations at the same time. This allows quicker change of PAN networks without the need to reconfigure.

The Bluetooth PAN dialog has three sub-menus to setup a Bluetooth dial-up connection:

  • Account
  • Advanced
  • IP Settings

Select a menu option to open the corresponding edit dialog, or press Back to save and exit the service details.
