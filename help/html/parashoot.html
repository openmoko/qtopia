ParaShoot

Objective: shoot the parachutists before they land.

  • Use / to rotate and aim the cannon.
  • Press to fire the cannon.
  • Press to exit.
  • Press the left / right arrows to position the cannon. Press the up arrow or OK to shoot. If three parachutists land the game is over.
  • Tap to start a new game.
  • Tap to exit the game.
