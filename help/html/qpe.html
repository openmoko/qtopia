This is the home screen.

From here you can:

  • Make Calls.
  • Enter a number then store it (saving to Contacts).
  • Enter someone's number and send them an SMS (using Messages).
  • Look-up a contact by entering their number or name.
  • Access other Qtopia functionality by selecting .

The home screen shows the current profile and other phone status such as if an alarm has been set.
