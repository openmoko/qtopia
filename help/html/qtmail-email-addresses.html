Addressing Email Messages

Enter one or more email addresses into the message addressing fields. The three fields are:

  • To - enter the addresses of recipients to receive the message.
  • CC - enter the addresses of recipients to receive courtesy copies.
  • BCC - enter the addresses of recipients to receive blind courtesy copies.

To address a message to multiple recipients, enter all the required email addresses on the appropriate address lines, separated by a comma.

If the CC or BCC fields are not displayed:

  • Select Fields, then CC field or BCC field as appropriate.
