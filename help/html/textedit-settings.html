Notes Settings Help

To reduce or enlarge the appearance of text:

  • Tap View, then Zoom in or Zoom out

Some documents are easier to view with a fixed-sized font:

  • Tap View, then Fixed-width to enable this.

To turn Wrap Lines on and off:

  • Tap View, then Wrap lines. (Wrap Lines causes long lines of text to be wrapped in the display so they can be seen fully.)

Zoom in: increases the text size.

Zoom out: decreases the text size.

Wrap lines: stops the text from disappearing off the screen.

Fixed-width: changes the typeface.

These settings can be changed for a note that is being viewed in edit mode.

  • Press and use to highlight Settings then press to select.
  • Use and to choose the Setting option from the menu.
