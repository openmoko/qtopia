During Calls

During calls, the current calls and the elapsed time are shown for each. The following options are available from the menu when a call is in progress:

Hold: puts the current caller on hold; you can make other calls with someone on hold.

Resume: resumes a held call.

Swap: takes one caller off hold and puts the other on hold.

End: ends the call.

End all calls: ends all calls, even those on hold.

Join: creates a group call from those in progress.

Split: makes the group call into one direct call and one on hold.

Transfer: puts the caller on hold through to another number.
