Asteroids - Bonuses

The following power bonuses are available:

  • Energy boost recharges the ship's energy supply.
  • Improves the ship's shields (it starts with no shields). Earning shield power bonuses increases the power of the shields.
  • Improves the ship's brakes (it starts with no brakes). Earning brake bonuses increases brake efficiency.
  • Improves the ship's firepower. A new ship can have 3 shots on screen at a time. Each firepower bonus increases the number of shots the ship can fire by 1.
  • Teleports you to a new random location on the screen.
