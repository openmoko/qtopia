Cleanup Wizard

This wizard allows you to free up some space on your by deleting old messages and events and by deleting unwanted documents. The wizard is accessible from the Options menu of the Storage tab in System Info.

Navigate through the wizard by pressing / or by clicking < Back and Next >. Press Back to cancel the wizard.

The following describe the main steps of the Cleanup Wizard

  • Preselection
  • Cleanup Messages
  • Cleanup Events
  • Cleanup Documents
  • Document Selection
  • Cleanup Progrses
  • Final Summary
