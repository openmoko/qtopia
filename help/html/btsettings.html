Bluetooth Help

The Bluetooth Settings application is used to access and control the functionality of your Bluetooth enabled device. You can control the power state, visibility and pairings of your Bluetooth device and also perform some common tasks using this utility.

To use the application, select the appropriate choice from the list. A new GUI page will be opened with additional options.
