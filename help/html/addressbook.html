Contacts Help

Contacts is used to store and retrieve names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers and other details for people you want to keep in contact with.

To find a contact when viewing the contacts list, spell the first few letters of the name. The list will be filtered accordingly.

To dial a contact, highlight their number then press . If the contact has only one number, that number will be dialed immediately. If the contact has more than one number, the contact record will be opened to allow you to select which number will be dialed.
