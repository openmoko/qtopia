Configure Contact List Headers

The Contact List view can be modified to display fields other than the phone number and e-mail address fields.

To change the contact number/e-mail field:
- Tap the dropdown list in the lower left corner of the screen
- Tap the field you want to view, e.g. Business Phone

To add/remove fields to/from the list:
- Tap View, then Configure headers
To Add a field:
- In the Available list, tap the field to add
- Tap the -> button to move the field into the Displayed list
To Remove a field:
- In the Displayed list tap the field to remove
- Tap the <- button to move the field into the Available list

The display widths of the columns can be adjusted using the Fit to width command.
