Language

Your Qtopia device can operate in a number of languages.

%%PHONE%%

Use / to select, then press .

When you change the language the dictionary also changes.

In the Menu, you can select secondary languages, which can be used for input. @@PHONE@@ %%PDA%%

To change the language:

  • Tap the Language settings icon .
  • A list of languages appears.
  • Tap the language of your choice.
  • Tap to save your changes, or to cancel.
@@PDA@@

Note: The Qtopia device may have to be restarted to complete the language change. This should only take a minute, during which the screen may show some black and white text.
