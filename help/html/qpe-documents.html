Documents

Documents such as Video and Audio clips, Notes, Pictures and Voice Notes can be created and/or viewed with various applications installed on your Qtopia device.

To view your documents:

  • Use to focus on and press Select to launch Documents.

To open a document with the application used to create it:

  • Use to and Select to select the document.
