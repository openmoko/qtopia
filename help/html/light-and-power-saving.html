Power Saving

Recommended settings for Battery mode:

  • Tick Dim light.
  • Tick Light off.
  • Tick Suspend.

Use the up/down arrows for each of these controls to set the number of seconds of inactivity that should elapse before dimming the back light, switching the light off entirely, and suspending the Qtopia device.

To prevent the device from powering down while connected to a network, tick Disable suspend while online.
