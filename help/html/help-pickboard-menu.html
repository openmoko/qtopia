Pickboard Menu

At the right of the pickboard is a pop up menu which offers an Add word option and a Reset option.

To add a word, tap each letter of the word in the letter groups in turn, then tap the menu arrow and tap Add. This will pop up the Add dialog which presents lists of letters. Tap the letters to compose your word, then tap the OK button to add your word to the dictionary.

To stop picking a word, tap the menu arrow and tap Reset.
