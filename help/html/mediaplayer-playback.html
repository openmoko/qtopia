Player Control

Play/Pause: press the Select button.

Stop: hold the Select button.

Skip: press the left or right button to skip forward or backward through a playlist.

Seek: hold the left or right button to seek forward or backward within a track.

Volume: hold the up or down button to increase or decrease volume.

Play/Pause: press the status icon in the top left corner of the window to switch between play and paused modes. The icon will change to show the new mode.

Stop: hold the status icon in the top left corner of the window to stop playback. The icon will change to show that playback is stopped.

Skip: drag the progress bar to the end of the current track to begin playing the next track.

Seek: drag the progress bar to seek forward or backward within the current track.

Volume: hold the progress bar until it turns into a volume bar, then drag left or right to lower/raise the volume.

Mute: select Mute On/Off from the Options menu to disable/enable sound.

Repeat: select Repeat from the Options menu to choose between three repeat settings:

  • repeat current song once
  • repeat entire playlist
  • no repeat

(Note: This feature does not work in Shuffle mode)

Track Details: view information about the current track by selecting Track Details from the Options menu

Settings: change settings for streaming media by selecting Settings from the Options menu.
