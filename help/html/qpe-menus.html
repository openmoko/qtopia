Options Menu

The Options menu provides a list of actions that may be performed in the current context.

Use the directional arrow buttons () to move the focus onto the menu item you want to activate, then press Select.

You can also press a corresponding number to activate a menu item. For example, press 1 to activate the first menu item.

To access the Options menu, simply tap on the "Options" label on the menu bar at the bottom of the screen.
