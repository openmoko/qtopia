Finding Contacts

To Find a Contact:

  • Tap Contact, then Find.
  • Enter the text you want to search for.
  • Contacts matching the text are displayed in the list.
  • Tap again to return to the full contacts list.

Once a contact has been assigned to a category the Contact List can be filtered to show only contacts assigned to a selected category.

See Also: Categories
